About Me

I graduated from Seton Hall University in 2011 with a BA in Public Relations. Since graduating I've been working in the field of Human Resources. I started as a Human Resources Generalist and now I work as a Benefits Analyst. I've always had an interest in learning more about developing websites. I started teaching myself HTML and CSS a few years ago, but I found it difficult to know what other languages I should learn without any guidance. I decided to make 2016 the year of Kelly. After years of trying to learn web development, I decided this was the year to throw myself into learning and do everything possible to change the course of my career, and that's how I ended up at Rutger's Coding Bootcamp.

Just a little more about me as a person. I am a problem solver, an outside the box thinker, an avid learner, an extremely hard worker. I have an insatiable drive to learn, so I walk around soaking up every piece information I can get my hands on. Music is the single most important thing in my life. I spend my time going to concerts, festivals, taking road trips, collecting vinyl, snuggling with my cats, and exploring everything around me.
